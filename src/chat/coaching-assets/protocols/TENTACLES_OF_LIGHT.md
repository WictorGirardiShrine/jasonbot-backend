## STEP 1 — Intro, framing and context

> "The mind will often choose to understand situations metaphorically. We can use this to our advantage."

### 1a. Pacing

> "Because many things in life seem to make demands on our energy. Doesn't it seem that way? People, tasks, commitments, there are many demands."

### 1b. Topic selection

Select a specific anxiety / memory / emotion / person that drains your energy.

## STEP 3 — Tentacles of energy or light

> "Try this — imagine yourself as a body of energy or light, and imagine that the energy drain you mentioned is pulling energy out of you. That shows up as a cord or tentacle of light leaving your body and going off somewhere. Imagine all the drains on your energy like that."

---

## STEP 4 — Select the biggest one

---

## STEP 5 — Pull and reclaim

Pull on it. Retract it into your body and reclaim that energy. Watch as it turns into white light when you own it. Keep pulling until you feel resistance.

---

## STEP 6 — Stretch

OK now pull some more, and notice how the cord of energy stretches and gets thinner.

---

## STEP 7 — Snap

Now keep pulling even more, until it snaps in two.

---

## STEP 8 — Reclaim your side

Pull your side of it back into yourself and reclaim the energy. After all, it's your energy in the first place, isn't it? And watch as the other side withers and falls away.

---

## STEP 9 — White-light frame

The energy you regain is white light energy.

## STEP 11 — Ask if better or worse

- If better, ask: are you sure? Because we can put it back the way it was if you want. Make them fight to keep it the new way.
- If worse, ask if it's that experience that got worse, or did that one get better and another experience popped up now that the first one was reduced? If another experience, do it again with the second experience. Don't do more than two in any one session.

---

## STEP 12 — Generalize and benediction

> "Now that you are free from that energy drain, you can discover that you now have more of your own energy to use. And you may want to channel that newly reclaimed energy into getting more of what you truly desire in your life. I don't know what that is for you, so I'll let you discover that for yourself. But I do know it will be beneficial and positive for you!"
